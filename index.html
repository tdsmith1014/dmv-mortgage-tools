Escrow Calculator

Calculates exact escrow months required at closing using the end-in-mind formula. Implements the 60-day rule, Maryland county-specific tax calendars, and NoVA locality due dates. Maryland seller proration (buyer pays seller) always shown. DC/VA arrears proration auto-calculated.

Closing Cost Calculator

Full closing cost engine with state-to-county transfer tax cascade, tax proration (DC/VA in arrears, MD prepaid), buyer and seller closing disclosure views, and a Reg Z APR calculator with legal tolerance checker (±0.125% fixed / ±0.250% ARM).

For home buyers
Buying a home in the DMV is complicated. These tools help you understand exactly what you will owe at closing — not just the down payment, but escrow reserves, prepaid taxes, transfer taxes, and insurance. No surprises. Just clear numbers before you sign.
For real estate professionals
Generate accurate closing cost estimates in seconds. The escrow calculator implements the end-in-mind formula with the 60-day rule, correct county tax calendars, and MD seller proration — so your estimates match what the settlement agent produces.

How these tools work

All calculations run entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

01
Select your jurisdiction
Choose DC, Maryland, or Virginia. For MD and NoVA, select your specific county or locality — each has different tax due dates, transfer tax rates, and escrow rules that affect your calculations.
02
Enter transaction details
Input purchase price, loan amount, closing date, annual taxes, and insurance. All fields update in real time — change one number and every calculation refreshes instantly.
03
Review your settlement summary
Each tool produces a color-coded settlement statement showing exactly what is due at closing, broken into prepaids, escrow reserves, and taxes — with a work-backwards explanation of how every number was calculated.
Disclaimer: These tools are for estimation purposes only and do not constitute a Loan Estimate, Closing Disclosure, or any other legally required disclosure.
